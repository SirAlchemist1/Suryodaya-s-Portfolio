About Me

Namaste! I'm Suryodaya Bikram Shahi, Nepali-born data scientist, systems tinkerer, and human-centered-AI believer.

While finishing my M.S. in Data Science at the University of Maryland, I'm spending this summer at Harvard Ophthalmology's AI & Robotics Lab, turning vision-language research into tools that help people see.

I build from first principles, compressing billion-parameter models onto edge devices and translating insights into products that serve classrooms and clinics back home.

Whether shipping code, publishing research, or mentoring teams at Kaushala, I chase one goal: technology that widens opportunity for everyone.
